About me

Hi, my name is Greg, but my pack name is Bloodfang. I never thought I'd be on one of these sites, but one of my friends met his packmate through here, so I figured why not give it a shot?
I was born and raised in what is now Slovenia, until I was chased away by a bunch of peasants with pitchforks and torches. Totally cliche, I know! During my time running from the Hunters, I really fell in love with
craft beer and skiing, so Denver seem like a natural place for me to wind up. Plus, everyone knows that Hipsters taste the best!
I'm searching for someone who would love to join me for skiing, a run, or chasing down a deer and eating its heart, but would be just as comfortable drinking beers on a patio. I'm a huge fan of Denver's music scene,
love to watch Broncos' games, and on occasion, go out and kill some Vampires.
If you'd like to get to know me better, feel free to reach out!
